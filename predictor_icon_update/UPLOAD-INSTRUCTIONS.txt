Upload these files to the root of your GitHub repository, replacing existing files where prompted:

1. icon.svg
2. apple-touch-icon.png
3. icon-512.png
4. manifest.webmanifest

Recommended: upload all four files.

For iPhone home screen icon, apple-touch-icon.png is the important one.
After uploading, delete the old Home Screen shortcut and add the app again from Safari.
